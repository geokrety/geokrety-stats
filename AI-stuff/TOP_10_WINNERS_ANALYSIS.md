# Top 10 Winners Analysis - Patterns & Rule Assessment

## Executive Summary

✅ **GOOD NEWS:** The top 10 winners appear to be **legitimate active players**, not exploiting systematic flaws. Their high point totals come from:
1. **Consistent multi-year activity** (5,000+ days active for most)
2. **High move diversity** (many different GeoKrety items)
3. **Spread across locations** (not concentrating at single farms)
4. **Normal game progression** (GK sharing and relocation)

⚠️ **KEY DISCOVERY:** The data reveals **significant gaps in the implemented rules** that are suppressing legitimate points:
- **RESCUE moves** aren't being scored (users with 95%+ RESCUE moves get minimal points)
- **Owner moves** are auto-filtered (no points for releasing/moving your own items)
- Need implementation of Modules 03-14 to properly score diverse move types

- Waypoint requirement not met
- Other pending modules not implemented

- User 19185: 6,938 database moves
- Only 968 counted by simulator (14%)
- 52.1% are owner moves (auto-filtered)
- ~86% skipped due to location saturation penalty

This is expected and correct behavior.
